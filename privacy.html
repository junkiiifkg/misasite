PRIVACY POLICY

About This Site

This is an unofficial fan site dedicated to MISA Esports. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to MISA Esports management or any of its official entities. All content is created by fans, for fans.

Data Collection

This site does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. There are no analytics, tracking scripts, cookies, or sign-up forms that send data externally.

Local Storage

This site uses your browser's localStorage to save:

  • Your chosen display name for the leaderboard
  • Misadle game progress (current day's guesses)
  • Leaderboard scores from mini-games

This data is stored only on your device and is never sent anywhere. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

Third-Party Services

This site loads resources from the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (Space Grotesk, Work Sans, Material Symbols). Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube Embeds — for video content. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN — for styling. No data is collected.

External Links

This site contains links to external websites (e.g., the official MISA Esports site, YouTube). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
